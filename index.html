INTRODUCTION:

Fatih Süleyman SOLMAZ; He was born in Turkey/Artvin in 1989. He completed his primary, secondary and high school education here.

"Undergraduate" at Atatürk University, Faculty of Fine Arts, Painting Department in 2013; In 2016, he received his master's degree from Atatürk University, Institute of Social Sciences, Painting Department, by presenting his thesis named "The Kitsch as Quality".

As an artist candidate, he has a great interest in digital media. In general, the fact that human's live together with the constantly developing and transforming digital world, and the bond they establish with technology in the production/consumption mechanism might provide resources for the digital environment. Therefore, this situation may be a reason for the possibility of art showing itself in different ways here. For this reason, he tries to establish a conceptual integrity between "Canvas Space" which can be felt as traditional, and "Digital Media" as part of the modern one.

In the light of all these general evaluations, he still continues to work on randomness and "Generative Art", so to speak.

Native language: Turkish, Second language: English(intermediate)
